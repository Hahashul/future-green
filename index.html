About Us

India's trusted
lighting partner.

Future Green builds LED lighting for every corner of a modern home and the spaces around it — from wafer-thin ceiling panels and adjustable COB spots to outdoor floods, street lights and decorative rope. Whatever the room, the promise is the same: high lumen efficiency, up to 80% energy saving, and a 2-year warranty on every fixture.
